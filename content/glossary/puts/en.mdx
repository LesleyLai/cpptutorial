---
title: "std::puts"
cppreference: "cpp/io/c/puts"
type: "C-style I/O Library"
token: "puts"
---

prints a null-terminated string and one additional newline character `'\n'` to the standard output.

Example usage:

```cpp notip=true
std::puts("Hello world!");
// Prints "Hello world!\n" to the screen"
```
